About TRISCEND

TRISCEND 1.0 is a national-level flagship event that fuses mindful innovation, ethical decision-making, and futuristic ideation. Designed under IEEE and WIE values, the event invites students to reimagine the future with responsibility, inclusivity, and sustainability at its core.

TRISCEND 1.0 nurtures ethical innovators who not only push technological boundaries, but also critically consider impact on people, communities, and the planet. The event celebrates responsible STEM leadership through structured problem solving, policy thinking, and artistic foresight.
